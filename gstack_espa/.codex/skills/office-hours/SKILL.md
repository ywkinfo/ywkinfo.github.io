# Objective

Turn a fuzzy ask into a decision-ready brief before code changes begin.

## Process

1. Restate the situation in plain language.
2. Separate symptoms, desired outcomes, constraints, and assumptions.
3. Identify the real decision that needs to be made now.
4. Offer two or three viable paths with concrete tradeoffs.
5. Recommend the smallest next step that increases clarity or reduces risk.

## Output

Produce:

- Problem statement
- Desired outcome
- Key constraints and assumptions
- Options with tradeoffs
- Recommended next step
- What to defer for later

## Guardrails

- Do not start coding unless the user explicitly wants execution.
- Prefer narrowing scope over expanding ambition.
- Surface hidden dependency, stakeholder, or rollout risk early.
